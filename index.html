What's Cosmic Calender?

The Cosmic Calendar is a method to visualize the vast history of the universe in which its 13.8 billion year lifetime is condensed down into a single year. In this visualization, the Big Bang took place at the beginning of January 1 at midnight, and the current moment is mapped onto the end of December 31 at midnight.
-Wikipedia


This website lets you explore the cosmic timeline, by converting the time into cosmic time and showing events that happened at that cosmic time. Confused? Click here
